About Us


Trio was founded in 2016 by a group of young individuals who are trying to make a difference in our community through social upliftment. We aim to empower young people with various skill sets ranging from unskilled to skilled workers. This is just one area we are passionate about, read on to find out more...


It was at the very beginning of the government’s implemented lockdown to mitigate the spread of Covid-19, when Trio realised that more had to be done for our community. The community was battling job loss, retrenchment, and no available alternatives. The question was, “how could Trio create job opportunities through food distribution?”

Trio is feeding the hungry on a daily basis. So far, Trio has fed 34 000 people thus far all over the Cape Flats and still counting. There are various distribution sites with cooks on standby for sponsorship

What differentiates us from other organisations is that we… We teach, transform and triumph in every aspect of the work we do. Giving back through life changing experiences
